Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about PlayGroundStrategy

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website, fill out the registration form with your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to start playing!

Is PlayGroundStrategy free to use?

Yes! PlayGroundStrategy offers a free tier that allows you to create teams, join public leagues, and access basic statistics. We also offer premium subscription plans with additional features and benefits for the most dedicated fantasy managers.

How do I join a league?

To join a league, log into your account, navigate to the "Leagues" section, and either join a public league or enter a private league code provided by the league commissioner. You can also create your own league and invite friends!

When do player statistics update?

Player statistics are updated in real-time during live matches. After a match concludes, final statistics are typically available within 30 minutes, following official validation from our data partners.

Can I use PlayGroundStrategy on my mobile device?

Absolutely! PlayGroundStrategy is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. We also offer dedicated iOS and Android apps for an enhanced mobile experience, available for download on the App Store and Google Play Store.

How can I recover my password?

To recover your password, click on the "Log In" button, then select "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
